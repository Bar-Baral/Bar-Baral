Bar-Baral

Bar-Baral is a Micronation, which is currently online, but may become physical later on.

About

Bar-Baral is an Oligarchy, but we do not hand power to those with wealth or influence, instead, Council members are picked if they are a good candidate for their position. Bar-Baral is run by a council with multiple branches, controlled by their respective leaders. We strive to create a place for everyone, where there is truly justice and peace.
